Why Work With A.J.F.?

Process

  1. Discovery & Planning - We begin by understanding your goals, audience, and vision. This phase involves discussing project requirements, defining scope, and identifying the best structure and features for your website. Proper planning ensures the project starts with clarity and direction.

  2. Design & Approval - Based on the initial planning, we create a visual direction that aligns with your brand. Layouts, color schemes, and typography are carefully crafted to reflect your identity. You’ll review the design and provide feedback before development begins, ensuring everything is aligned from the start.

  3. Development - Once the design is approved, we bring it to life through clean, responsive, and optimized code. The website is built to perform smoothly across devices and browsers, with attention to structure, functionality, and performance.

  4. Testing & LaunchTesting & Launch - Before launch, the website undergoes thorough testing to ensure functionality, responsiveness, and performance. After final checks and client approval, the site is deployed and made live, ready for real-world use.

  5. Support & Maintenance - After launch, we continue to support your website through updates, optimizations, and maintenance services. This ensures your site remains secure, up-to-date, and performing at its best as your needs evolve.
